GEO Question Matrix

We translate industry traffic into "answerable questions" by mapping the buyer's core question tree.

  • How to choose? (brand, model, specs)
  • How to buy? (price, manufacturer, authenticity)
  • How to use? (installation, maintenance, optimization)
  • How to avoid pitfalls? (model analysis, budget matching)
  • How to compare? (features, appearance, results)
  • How to verify results? (case studies, reviews)

GEO Question Matrix Formula

Value = Search Demand × Answer Accuracy × Provability × Deliverability

The higher the opportunity and the more significant the decision, the higher the content value — these questions generate high traffic and commercial value.
